Nucleus V1

Keeps phones, tablets, and laptops connected when cell towers and Wi-Fi aren't around. Nucleus V1 runs both Wi-Fi and LoRa radios, giving you short-range speed and long-range reach in the same unit.

Out of the box Nucleus V1 creates an encrypted WiFi mesh for IP connectivity and to provide a pathway for the onboard Reticulum transport instance, plus a dedicated LoRa radio for Meshtastic. Share maps, chat, and ATAK data miles away without the internet.
