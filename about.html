About Natak Mesh

Learn about our company, our mission, and the innovative networking solutions we provide.

Our Story

Natak Mesh was originally inspired by the Rocket M5 MANET builds done by the team at teamawarenesskit.org. We used those solutions for a while but wanted something simpler to set up and use.

Our journey led us to Meshtastic, but the extremely low throughput pushed us back towards WiFi-based solutions. We worked for most of a year on a HaLow WiFi build, but the currently available low-power radios did not have enough of a range advantage to make up for the low throughput.

This exploration ultimately led to our current approach: a combination of standard WiFi and LoRa to have the best of both worlds—high throughput where possible via WiFi, while still maintaining long-range LoRa connections for situations where WiFi is out of range or blocked.

Our Mission & Values

At Natak Mesh, our mission is to provide affordable networking solutions that enable reliable connectivity without requiring existing infrastructure. We focus on giving you the essential capabilities of high-end radios at a price that actually makes sense using standard, off-the-shelf components and open source software rather than locked-down proprietary systems. By sticking to simple, proven software and hardware, we make advanced networking capabilities accessible to everyone—eliminating the barriers of both expensive commercial solutions and complex DIY projects that require extensive technical expertise.

Affordability First

Using commercial off-the-shelf components to create cost-effective solutions that make advanced networking accessible to more users. Our affordable approach enables deploying more nodes across your area, creating denser mesh networks with better coverage and reliability than fewer, more expensive units.

Infrastructure Independence

Designing systems that create robust, self-healing networks in any environment without requiring existing infrastructure.

Practical Innovation

Leveraging modern technologies like 3D printing for custom case designs, Meshtastic, Reticulum, and other networking protocols to create hardware that offers multiple connectivity options. We combine proven approaches with effective solutions to deliver practical, cost-effective results.

Security Without Compromise

Implementing strong encryption and security features without adding unnecessary complexity or cost. Our approach ensures your data remains protected while maintaining the affordability and ease of use of our systems.

Communication Philosophy

Our mesh nodes represent more than just networking hardware—they're an open communication platform designed to adapt to your specific needs. We believe in providing you with a toolbox rather than a single-purpose solution.

Each node comes equipped with three distinct communication capabilities that you can use independently or in any combination:

  • IP Mesh Networking: A robust, general-purpose network foundation that supports any standard networking application
  • Reticulum Integration: Access to secure, non-IP based communications for privacy-focused applications
  • Standalone Meshtastic: Independent LoRa communication that works with the broader Meshtastic ecosystem

The beauty of this approach is flexibility. Whether you need high-bandwidth IP connectivity, ultra-secure messaging, long-range LoRa communication, or all three simultaneously, the choice is yours. You're not locked into a single communication method or vendor ecosystem.

This open architecture means our nodes can fill countless niches—from emergency communications to IoT deployments—all while giving you the tools to expand and customize functionality as your needs evolve.
