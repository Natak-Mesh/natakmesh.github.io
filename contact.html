Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our products and services.

How do I purchase Natak Mesh products?

Natak Mesh products can be purchased directly through our online store. For bulk orders or custom solutions, please contact our sales team at sales@natakmesh.com.

What kind of support do you offer?

We offer comprehensive support for all our products, including technical assistance, troubleshooting, and firmware updates. Our support team is available via email at support@natakmesh.com.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship our products internationally. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Please note that customers are responsible for any import duties or taxes that may apply.

What is your warranty policy?

All Natak Mesh hardware products come with a standard 1-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. Extended warranty options are available for purchase. For more details, please refer to our warranty policy document.

Can I integrate Natak Mesh products with my existing network?

Yes, Natak Mesh products are designed to integrate seamlessly with existing network infrastructure. Our documentation provides detailed guides for various integration scenarios, and our support team is available to assist with any specific requirements.
