Our Products

WiFi, Meshtastic, and Reticulum in ready-to-deploy hardware

Mesh V1

Multi-protocol mesh networking node combining WiFi mesh, Meshtastic, and Reticulum in a single platform. Once configured, nodes automatically form secure networks across all three protocols, providing high-speed WiFi connectivity alongside dedicated Meshtastic mesh networking.

Key Features

  • Automatic Mesh Formation - Once configured, nodes instantly discover and connect to form self-healing WiFi networks
  • Dual-Radio Architecture - High-powered Wi-Fi (2.4/5.8 GHz) and long-range LoRa (915/868 MHz)
  • Multi-Protocol Support - IP networking, Reticulum, and Meshtastic protocols
  • WPA3 Encrypted Mesh - Secure 802.11s mesh with batman-adv routing
  • Commercial Off-the-Shelf Design - Affordable, repairable, and open source
  • Low Power & Durable - 5V operation with compact, rugged design

ATAK Compatibility

  • Seamless ATAK Operations - Once connected to the WiFi mesh, ATAK devices automatically discover and communicate with other ATAK units across the network
  • High-Speed Communications - WiFi mesh enables high data rate connections for location sharing, chat, pictures, voice, and real-time communications between ATAK devices
  • Extended Range ATAK Connectivity - Onboard Meshtastic node integrates with the official Meshtastic ATAK plugin to provide long-range ATAK connectivity in poor RF conditions
